Project Description        

vQuiz is an app (Android) to generate quizzes with multiple-choice questions, in its first stage on Health topics. It is an Educational Game, ideal for students of medicine, nursing, or other careers in the health area.

With more than 25,000 questions, equivalent to hundreds of exams, available from your mobile device, at all times. That will guarantee hours and hours of preparation and entertainment. By using vQuiz you will not need to make copies of old exams, thereby saving money and taking care of the environment.

  • vQuiz allows you to evaluate yourself on:
  • Health topics
  • Body parts
  • Diseases
  • Treatments
  • Diagnostics
  • Clinical cases



Project Status

  • 1st Stage. Beta version operational
  • Implementation of the mobile application type trivia on Android, as a tool to complement the study
  • It allows to generate multiple selection quizzes from a Bank of 25,000 questions about the Health Area
  • Allows self-assessment
  • Rate the quizzes, show the correct answer, comment and/or justification for the answer
  • Generate graphs of user behavior for each topic
  • It saves time and money, while being an environmentally friendly App

  1. 2nd Stage what we are working on
  2. Implementation of a Socket Server
  3. Allow to share the results of a topic with other users
  4. Allow you to compare your own results with the rest of the world
  5. Allow the application of remote assessments
  6. Allow an instructor to observe the behavior of a group of students in real time
  7. Expand the Question Bank
  8. Publish the App in the Play Store, with a subscription model

  • 3rd Stage in Planning state
  • Implement the App in IOS
  • Transform the user experience to make it more like a video game



What do we require?

To continue with the development of the project we are looking for funding, for this we are allowing the pre-launch use of the App.



What do we offer pre-launch?

Access to generate quizzes from the Question Bank, with more than 25,000 questions distributed by different topics; access to the results and graphics of the completed quizzes; access to the correct answer, and access to the comment, or justification of the answer, for 365 days for the collaboration.
